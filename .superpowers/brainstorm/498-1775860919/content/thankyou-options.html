After submitting the form — what happens?

Two approaches for the "Thank You" experience. Which feels right for 77Rentals?

A

Inline swap — same section, no page change

The form fades out and a thank-you message fades in right where the form was. User stays on the page, can keep scrolling to see properties, amenities, etc.

Form section — after submit
✨
¡Gracias!
Nuestro equipo te contactará pronto.
Revisa tu WhatsApp y correo electrónico.

WhatsApp

Pros

  • No navigation — user stays in flow
  • Can still scroll down to browse properties
  • Simpler to build

Cons

  • Can't share a "Thank you" URL
  • Less dramatic impact
B

Dedicated page — /gracias route

Submitting the form navigates to a full thank-you page at /gracias. Feels more formal and intentional. Can include CTA buttons to go back or jump to properties.
